I've booked for NHS Hack Day - What Now?

Here are some things you can do right now:
  • Sign up for our Google Group here, which will keep you updated about the event, and enable you to become part of the NHS Hack Day Community

  • Read the Frequently Asked Questions section of the website, so you know what to expect when you get here

  • Read about previous NHS Hack Day projects in the 'Events Teams and Projects' section - we're always pleased when previous good work can be reused, revamped, updated, extended, forked, or otherwise made something of.

  • Book your accommodation and transport, if needed

  • Introduce yourself on the Google Group, and talk about ideas you have or problems you face as a patient/carer/clinician/health IT developer

  • Tweet about NHS Hack Day, spread the word!
